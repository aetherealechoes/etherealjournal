What Are the Lunar Mansions?
ما هي منازل القمر؟

The Lunar Mansions are an ancient Arabic astronomical system that divides the full zodiac circle (360°) into 28 equal stations. The Moon passes through each mansion over the course of one to three days on its monthly journey through the sky.

Arab astronomers of the Golden Age used these mansions to understand the prevailing energy of each day and to choose optimal timing for all manner of activities — from agriculture and travel to marriage and commerce.

Each mansion carries a stellar or planetary name, a distinct energy, a nature (auspicious or inauspicious), and a planetary ruler. This guide takes you through all 28 mansions so you can understand how the Moon's daily energy influences your life and decisions.

🌙 How to Use This Guide

Find the Moon's current mansion (use our app or a lunar calendar), then read that mansion's description and let its guidance shape your day.

⭐ Auspicious & Inauspicious

Auspicious mansions (Saad) are blessed times for starting and planning. Inauspicious mansions (Nahs) are not evil — they are simply times for reflection, caution, and inner awareness.

🔥 The Four Elements

Each mansion belongs to one of four elements: Fire (enthusiasm and initiative), Water (emotion and intuition), Air (thought and communication), or Earth (work and material matters).

🪐 Planetary Rulers

Each mansion has a ruling planet that colors its character. Mars lends courage, Venus brings beauty, Mercury grants eloquence — each ruler adds its own signature quality.

New beginnings and fresh intentions. A powerful time to plant seeds, set goals, and clarify what you wish to call in.

Take your first steps and build momentum. The energy set at the New Moon begins to move — act on your intentions.

Face obstacles with courage. This is a decision point — commit to your path and push through what stands in your way.
🌔

Refine and trust the process. You are close — make small adjustments, stay patient, and have faith in what is unfolding.
🌕

ILLUMINATED
Illumination and fulfillment. The moon reveals what was hidden — celebrate your progress, release what no longer serves, and receive insights.
🌖
Waning Gibbous
GRATEFUL
Share your wisdom and practice gratitude. Give back what you have received and honor the lessons this cycle has brought.
🌗
Last Quarter
RELEASING
Let go and forgive. Release what no longer serves you to make space for what is coming in the new cycle ahead.
🌘
Waning Crescent
RESTING
Rest and surrender. Replenish your energy and reflect deeply. The cycle completes itself, preparing you to begin again.

Your Daily Practice with the Mansions
الممارسة اليومية

The simplest way to integrate the Lunar Mansions into your daily life is to spend a few minutes each morning connecting with the energy of the day. This guide becomes your steady teacher on that journey.

1

Find today's Lunar Mansion

Open our app or a lunar calendar to discover which of the 28 mansions the Moon occupies today. Each mansion lasts one to three days.

2

Read the mansion's description

Open this guide and locate the current mansion. Read its description thoughtfully and ask: How does this energy apply to my life today?

3

Notice the nature — Auspicious or Inauspicious

Auspicious mansions call for action and new beginnings. Inauspicious mansions are not bad — they are simply times for reflection, caution, and patience. Work wisely with each.

4

Write your intention in your journal

Set aside a few lines in your journal to record what you want to create, learn from, or release in light of this mansion's energy.

5

Review in the evening

Before sleep, reflect on your day. Was the mansion's energy palpable? What did you notice? These observations deepen your relationship with the Moon over time.
